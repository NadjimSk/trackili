Privacy Policy — Trackili

Last updated: 22 August 2025

At Trackili, we value and prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application and website.

1) Who we are & scope

Data Controller: Trackili (individual developer).
Contact for privacy requests: contact@trackili.app

This policy applies to the Trackili app and website. We collect the minimum data needed to provide the service, we are transparent about how we use it, and we do not sell your personal data.

2) What we collect and why (and the legal basis)

  • Account data: your email and (optionally) display name, plus your Firebase user ID.
    Purpose: create and manage your account, sign-in, support communications and confirming sensitive requests (e.g., account deletion).
    Legal basis: Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Parcel tracking numbers: the numbers you add to see shipment status and updates.
    Purpose: fetch and show your shipment status, and help you restore your list when changing devices.
    Legal basis: Contract performance.
  • User preferences: language, notification settings, country/currency to display AliExpress prices for your region.
    Purpose: customize your experience.
    Legal basis: Contract performance / Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)), as applicable.
  • Diagnostics & performance (Crashlytics and Analytics): technical information like Android version, device model, app version, crash logs, and some aggregated usage metrics.
    Purpose: fix issues, improve stability, security, and quality. We do not use this for behavioral advertising and we do not collect precise location.
    Legal basis: Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)). We do not currently provide an in-app toggle; you may object by contacting us and we will honor your request as far as practicable.
  • Push notification tokens (FCM):
    Purpose: send the notifications you enable (parcel progress / deals).
    Legal basis: Consent (withdrawable at any time in the app or device settings).
  • Support communications: content of your messages and your contact details.
    Purpose: respond and improve the service.
    Legal basis: Contract performance / Legitimate interests.

We do NOT collect: full legal name, postal address, phone number, payment information, precise location, or access to camera/microphone/contacts for tracking purposes.

3) How we use your data

  • Operate your account and sign you in.
  • Save and display the tracking numbers you add and retrieve the latest statuses.
  • Send the notifications you enabled (you can turn them off any time).
  • Diagnose crashes and improve performance (no behavioral ad targeting).
  • Show affiliate banners/links to external products; clicking them takes you to the third party’s policies.

We do not make automated decisions that produce legal effects concerning you.

4) Sharing with trusted parties (processors/recipients)

  • Google Firebase: Authentication (Auth), Cloud Messaging (FCM), Crashlytics and Analytics. We share the minimum necessary to run these features.
  • Shipment information providers: we only send the tracking number to retrieve the shipment status.
  • Price/deals providers (e.g., AliExpress APIs): we use product identifiers to fetch details/prices.

We do not sell your data and we do not share it for behavioral advertising.

5) Ads & affiliate links

Trackili may display affiliate links/banners. We may earn a commission if you purchase via those links at no extra cost to you. Prices/availability are determined by the third party; please review their policies and terms before purchasing.

6) Retention & deletion

  • Account, tracking numbers, preferences, and FCM token: kept while your account is active and deleted immediately when you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Support messages: up to 12 months from your last contact (or deleted upon request).
  • Crashlytics/Analytics: crash data and metrics for an operationally necessary period (e.g., 90–180 days for Crashlytics, and up to 14 months for aggregated metrics where applicable).
  • Automated backups (if any): removed as part of the deletion cycle and not used to restore individually deleted data.

Account & data deletion:
In-app: Settings → Delete Account (immediate deletion from our operational systems).
On the web (alternative): /terms/delete-account.html

7) Your choices & controls

  • Notifications: turn parcel/deal notifications on/off in the app or in your device settings.
  • Crashlytics/Analytics: used under legitimate interests to improve quality. No in-app toggle currently; you can object by contacting us and we will honor your request as far as practicable.
  • Manage your data: you can add/edit/delete tracking numbers and update your display name in the app.

8) Your rights (especially in the EEA/UK)

Depending on your local law, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object (especially to processing based on legitimate interests), portability, and withdraw consent without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

How to exercise: email contact@trackili.app and we will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

9) Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures (e.g., TLS in transit, least-privilege access controls, internal error reviews). No method is 100% secure, and we will provide notices as required by law in case of a relevant incident.

10) International transfers

Your data may be processed outside the EEA (e.g., via Firebase). Where applicable, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and appropriate safeguards.

11) Children

The service is not directed to children under 16 in the EEA (or under the local legal age elsewhere). If we learn we collected a child’s data unintentionally, we will delete it upon notice.

12) Cookies (website)

Our website may use essential cookies (e.g., language/security). We do not use behavioral advertising cookies. If we add web analytics in the EEA, we will update this policy accordingly.

13) External links

When you navigate to shipping companies or stores, their own policies and terms apply. Please review them before sharing any information.

14) Disclaimer (important)

We are not a shipping company and we do not generate tracking data; we display it as provided by external services. Updates may be delayed or differ across systems. For shipment issues, please contact your carrier directly.

For AliExpress data: we display prices/deals as received from external providers and they may not always be accurate; verify the final price and seller terms on the store before purchasing. We are not responsible for purchase decisions based on outdated information.

15) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will show the “Last updated” date at the top and may send an in-app notice for material changes. Your continued use of Trackili means you accept the updated version.
