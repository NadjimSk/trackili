Delete Your Trackili Account

If you no longer wish to use Trackili, you can permanently delete your account from within the app or by contacting support.

Can’t access the app?

You can request account deletion by email. Please follow these steps so we can verify ownership quickly:

  1. Send an email to support@trackili.app.
  2. Use the same email address you used to register in the app.
  3. Subject: Delete my Trackili account.
  4. Optional: include any additional info that helps us verify (e.g., approximate signup date).

Once verified, we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours. This action is irreversible.

How to Delete Your Account from the App

You can delete your Trackili account directly from the app at any time.
Go to Menu → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete my Account → confirm the action.

Deleting your account will remove your profile and all related data from Trackili. We do not retain backups after deletion.
